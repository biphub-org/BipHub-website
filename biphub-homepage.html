For students

How it works

Three steps from finding a BIP to landing in your destination country — fully funded.

1

Find

Filter BIPs by country, field of study, language and dates. Save your favorites and compare options that match your degree.

2

Apply

Apply through your home university's Erasmus+ office using the contact info on the BIP page. We make the matchmaking easy.

3

Go

Complete the virtual component online, then travel for the physical mobility — fully funded by Erasmus+ at €79/day plus travel.
